## What The Product Is

NEXUS is a support-to-engineering investigation product for recurring customer-facing outages.

It is strongest when presented as:

- a support-triage workflow
- with bounded replay validation
- bounded debugging guidance
- runtime-aware mitigation ranking
- explicit human review

## What Not To Use As The Main Story

Do not lead with:

- “AI ops platform”
- “autonomous agents”
- “universal incident response”

Lead with:

- reduced manual relay
- faster, stronger escalations
- better engineering handoff quality
